My name is Sarah Muir. I was born in Edmonton, AB, Canada. Thus, it is very lucky that my favourite season is winter. Another thing that I enjoy is piano. One of my favourite songs to play as a child was “Misty” by Johnny Mathis. As I got a wee bit taller, I began to focus more on classical music instead of jazz.
Something that has remained incredibly consistent are my favourite movies. “Treasure Planet”, “Atlantis: the Lost Empire”, and “The Princess and the Frog” have always made me gasp, and giggle no matter how many times I watch them.
